Ureña Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

1. Site Name & Logo

Site Name

Ureña Chamber of Commerce — a business community hub for the border city of Ureña, Táchira, Venezuela.

Logo

The logo is a simple icon representing local identity and professionalism. It is displayed in the header of every page alongside the site name.

  images/favicon.png

2. Site Purpose

The Ureña Chamber of Commerce website serves as the official digital presence for local businesses in Ureña, Táchira, Venezuela. Its primary purposes are:

  • Provide a searchable directory of member businesses with contact information.
  • Announce community events and networking opportunities.
  • Feature member spotlights to promote Gold and Silver tier businesses.
  • Offer an easy way for businesses to join the chamber and grow their network.
  • Serve as a reliable information source about Ureña's business ecosystem.

3. Target Audience

🏪 Local Business Owners

Entrepreneurs in Ureña who want to grow their customer base and network with other businesses in the region.

🛍️ Consumers & Residents

Locals and visitors looking for trusted businesses, services, and upcoming community events in the city.

📦 Wholesale & Border Traders

Cross-border traders who rely on Ureña's commercial activity and need to find reliable suppliers and partners.

🏛️ Government & Institutions

Municipal offices and NGOs interested in connecting with the local business community for development projects.

7. Home Page Wireframe

The wireframe below shows the planned layout for the Chamber Home page (index.html). It follows a mobile-first design with responsive columns for tablets and desktops.

Layout Description

  • Header: Logo + site name on the left, hamburger menu button on mobile.
  • Navigation: 4 links — Home, Discovery, Directory, Join. Horizontal on desktop, collapsible on mobile.
  • Hero Banner: Full-width gradient section with headline, sub-text, and CTA button.
  • Info Cards: 3 cards in a row — current date, weather, member count.
  • Home Grid (2-col on desktop):
    • Left — Upcoming Events (3 event cards)
    • Right — Member Spotlights (2–3 random Gold/Silver members)
  • Footer: Address, copyright, last modified date.
